Frequently asked questions

A few common questions electricians ask before getting started.

Is there a minimum contract?

Yes. All plans include a 12-month minimum agreement. This allows us to build your site properly and support it long-term rather than treating it as a short-term project.

Do I pay anything upfront?

For most electrician websites, there is no upfront build fee. You simply start your monthly plan. If your project is larger or more complex, we’ll discuss this clearly beforehand.

What happens after 12 months?

After the initial 12-month term, your plan continues monthly. You’re not locked into another long fixed contract.

Can I keep my domain name?

Yes. You remain in control of your domain. We can help you connect or transfer it, but ownership stays with you.
