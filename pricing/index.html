Pricing

Simple, predictable monthly pricing.

One main plan that covers your website, hosting, updates and support – so you always know what you’re paying, and what you’re getting.

WebHaven Plan

Ideal for most small businesses and service-based companies.

£29.99 /month

No upfront build fee for a limited number of clients.

Included

  • Full website design & build (typically up to X pages – home, services, about, contact, etc.).
  • Managed hosting, security updates and backups.
  • Reasonable content changes each month (text, photos, opening hours, etc.).
  • Basic on-page SEO setup and structure.

Fair use & notes

  • “Reasonable changes” means small updates, not a full redesign every month. If a bigger project is needed, we’ll discuss options first.
  • Domain name cost is usually separate (you can keep control of it), but we’ll help you set it up or transfer it.
  • If you need something larger or more complex, we can quote a custom plan.

Growing beyond the basics

As WebHaven grows, you might introduce different plans for different types of clients – for example, a higher tier for larger sites or more frequent updates. For now, one clear plan keeps your offer simple.

Larger website projects

For bigger sites with more pages or advanced functionality, you can quote a custom build fee or a higher monthly rate.

High-change businesses

If a client needs constant updates, you could create a higher plan that includes more frequent content changes.

Add-on services

Things like blog writing, ad landing pages or advanced SEO could be offered as add-ons when you’re ready.

Not sure what to charge yet? Start with a fair monthly price for your local market, then adjust over time as you get more experience and see what clients find most valuable.
